Privacy Policy for As A Fractions

Effective Date: [2-9-2024]

Welcome to As A Fractions. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from you when you visit our website, how we use that information, and the measures we take to protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use As A Fractions, we may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information:
Personal information is any information that can be used to identify you as an individual. This may include your name, email address, and other contact details that you voluntarily provide when you subscribe to our newsletter, fill out a contact form, or engage in any other interaction with our website.

b. Non-Personal Information:
Non-personal information includes data that does not identify you directly. This can include technical information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, and browsing behavior on our website. We collect this information through cookies and similar technologies to help us improve our website and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: We use your information to operate and enhance the features of As A Fractions and to ensure that our website meets your needs.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your email address to respond to your inquiries, send you updates, newsletters, or promotional materials if you have opted in to receive them.
  • To Analyze Website Usage: Non-personal information is used to analyze trends and user behavior on our website, which helps us improve the user experience and develop new features.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

As A Fractions uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize you when you return to our website.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functionality of our website and cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect information about how visitors use our website, allowing us to improve its functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: These allow us to remember your preferences and enhance your experience on our site.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used by third-party services, such as Google AdSense, to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

4. Third-Party Advertising

We may partner with third-party advertising networks, such as Google AdSense, to display advertisements on our website. These third-party advertisers may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your activities on our website and other websites to provide you with targeted advertising based on your interests.

What This Means for You:

  • Personalized Ads: You may see ads tailored to your browsing behavior and preferences based on the information collected by third-party advertisers.
  • Opting Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ad Settings page (https://adssettings.google.com) or by managing your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Please note that we do not control these third-party cookies and encourage you to review the privacy policies of these advertisers to learn more about their data collection practices and opt-out mechanisms.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We will share your information with third parties if you have given us explicit permission to do so.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a legal request, such as a subpoena, court order, or government demand.
  • To Protect Our Rights: We may share information if we believe it is necessary to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or others.

6. Security of Your Information

We take your privacy seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

As A Fractions is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
